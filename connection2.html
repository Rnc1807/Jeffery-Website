MY PERSONAL CONNECTION

I have skied since i was young first at Happy Valley in whakapapa and then at turoa.
My family goes skiing yearly at turoa and I find it a lot of fun.
Recently I skied at mount hutt t in the south island and it was a blast.
I hope that skiing will not be stopped by global warming so the next generation can experience it.
I also hope that skiing doesn't become too commercialised
otherwise it would take away the special feeling
