SKIING IN NEW ZEALAND

Skiing was first introduced to nz through norway gold miners who used skiing as a mode of
transport to get to their mines. The first ski club to be set up was at mt ruapehu.
The first commercial ski field was coronet peak when a ski tow was put in.
Currently there are 25 ski resorts in New Zealand 18 of which are commercial fields.
There are three ski fields in the north island whakapapa,turoa and mangunui.
Whakapapa has recently got a gondola-the sky waka gondola to take you up.
