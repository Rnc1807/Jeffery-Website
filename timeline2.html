5000 years ago: Hunters from the altai region
in Asiae are believed to hunt reindeer and elk herds using skis.

18th century: Skis were used by scandinavian farmers, hunters and
warriors during the middle ages for various tasks

Pre 1840: the cambered ski is invented in the province
of telemark the camber was bow shaped ski
which allowed the skiers weight to be distributed evenly.

1882: the first skis made out of hickory is made.
1893: the first two layer laminated ski is made in Norway.
1932: the first successful three layer laminated skis are made in Norway
1939: The first ski lift is installed on the sun valley ski resort

1940: the first t-bar is installed
