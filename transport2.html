MODES OF TRANSPORT

There are 4 main modes of transport to get up
the mountain: t-bar , pommer, rope pull, chairlifts and more recently a gondola.
The first ski lift was at proctor mountain in the ski resort sun valley idaho in 1939.
The first t-bar was installed in 1940,Now almost all ski resorts have ski chairlifts.
Ski lifts are basically a bunch of carriages attached to a metal wire that
gets moved around in an elongated circle by wheels when the seat gets to the dismounting area it
is transferred on to a separate circuit that is slower to make it easier to dismount.
You sit on a seat on the carriages.
